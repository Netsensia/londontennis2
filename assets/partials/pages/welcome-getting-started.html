Welcome to London Tennis, the biggest tennis community in London! Now that you've created your account you may wish to take a moment to read this brief overview of how you can get the most out of the site!

Manage your profile

The 'My Account' tab on the main menu will allow you to view and update all aspects of your profile including your playing preferences, your availability calendar, favourite courts, email preferences and login details.

In order to start appearing in the player lists you should write a bit about yourself in your player profile.

Find Players

Click the 'Players' menu tab to view a list of London Tennis players. Click on any column on this list to sort by your preferred criteria (click the same column a second time to reverse the sort order). You can also use the sub menu links such as 'Most Played' and 'Recent Joiners' to sort the list.

While on the 'Players' page you will see the 'Player Search' option on the sub menu bar. You can use this form to perform a detailed player search.

Use the Calendar link on the same sub menu to view the availability of other players looking for matches over the next few weeks. Don't forget to keep your own calendar up-to-date if you wish for other people to find you in this manner!

You should also notice the 'Buddy List' link on the sub menu. You can use this to keep track of your friends and favourite players. You will see your match record against each friend and you can choose to receive email notifications when certain of your friends post a match result.

Message Players

You can contact any player through the London Tennis website by clicking the 'Send a message to this player' link next to their name at the top of their full profile.

If you see the mini-profile pop up box when you click a player's name, there is a link towards the bottom of the box which will take you to their full profile page.

Record your scores

All scores between London Tennis members can be recorded on the site. Simply use the 'Add Result' link on the 'Results' sub menu to record one of your own results. You can also use the 'Results' tab to view the results of all other friendly and competitive matches.

Once you have completed matches against five unique opponents you will be awarded a London Tennis ranking. A full breakdown of the ranking system we used can be found in the Knowledgebase by selecting the 'Help' tab.

Results between LTA registered players will be forwarded to the LTA to count towards your LTA rating. If you have an LTA number you can add this on the 'Playing Standard' sub option under the 'My Account' menu.

Find Courts

Click the 'Courts' tab to search our comprehensive courts database.

Please take a moment to find your favourite courts and add them to your profile using the 'Add to Favourites' link on the court's details page.

Please keep us up-to-date with any knowledge you have about courts and venues that is not reflected in the database.

Join a Competition

There are four regionalised league seasons per year and two all-regions knockout competitions. News on competitions is usually displayed either in the scrolling ticker on the home page or in the news box just below the ticker.

Rules and information about the running of the competitions can be found using the sub menu of the competitions page where you can also sign up for a competition. On this sub menu you can also find the results of all past competitions.

The finals of the bi-annual knockout tournaments are held at the fantastic National Tennis Centre
