The finals of the London Tennis 2011 Summer Knockout Tournament were staged at the heart of British professional tennis, the National Tennis Centre on Sunday 19th September 2010.

The Mens' final was postponed due to one of the finalists being unavailable, while in the Ladies' final, James Linney (linney13) beat Antonio Agresta (agre07) 6-4 7-5.

Many thanks to the umpire, Tim Pearce, and to the photographer of those amazing photos, Derek Linney, (www.takebetterpictures.co.uk). Derek is James' father.
