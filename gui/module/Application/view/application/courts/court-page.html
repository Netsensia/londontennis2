Wimbledon park tennis courts are run by Merton council and there are 20 of them in total. There are 10 artificial grass which are in great condition and 10 macadam courts which are in fair to good condition.

Booking line is 020 8879 0611 (this is only open peak times and between 9-9.30am Mon -Fri.), non-members can only book on the day and members who enjoy slightly reduced court fees can book up to one week in advance, to join one must visit the booking hut to get an application form, membership is Â£35.

There are several licensed park coaches, one of the coaches is Paul Barton who runs coaching courses and private lessons for players of all ages and standards, his contact number is 07961 170675.
